ABOUT US

Life-Saver is a Community-run and maintained Non-profitable Organization for the care of people suffering from the unavailability of oxygen. The organization was founded way back on 1st April, 2021 and has since been working tirelessly towards the goal of making these people love life. At Life-Saver, we are on a mission to bring the joy of life to those people.
